    Great scenario! You're essentially building a purchase system where:

Products are in a trunks table.

Buyers and owners are in the participents table.

When someone (a buyer) buys a product from a trunk, they:

Pay the owner (identified via participents_id in trunks).

Have their amount reduced.

The owner gets the money.

The purchase is logged in the transaction table.
