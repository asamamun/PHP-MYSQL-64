Anonymous Suggestion Box for Organizations
Concept: Internal feedback system for companies, schools, or clubs where members can submit suggestions or concerns anonymously.
Features:
Organization-specific registration and login.
Anonymous suggestion submission.
Admin dashboard for reviewing suggestions.
Voting on popular suggestions (can be public/private).



Digital Time Capsule
Concept: Users can write messages or upload files that will be "locked" and emailed or revealed to them after a specific date.
Features:
User account with capsule creation.
Upload text, images, or files.
Set open date (e.g., 5 years later).
Scheduled email delivery or dashboard unlock.



Skill Progress Tracker for Students
Concept: Instead of grades, students log specific skills (e.g., HTML, data analysis) and track proficiency levels with visual growth charts.

Features:
Student profiles with skill trees.
Teacher dashboard to approve progress.
Gamification with badges or level-ups.
Export skill report as PDF/resume.



Micro-Investment Idea Pitch Board
Concept: A platform where users pitch small-scale startup ideas (<$500 budget) and others can "invest" micro-amounts or offer help.
Features:
Idea posting with budget and expected ROI.
Users can invest, vote, or comment.
Track investment commitments.
Dashboard for active projects and ROI tracking.
